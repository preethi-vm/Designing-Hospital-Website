CARDIOLOGY DEPARTMENT

        


The Department of Cardiology at WE CARE was established under the leadership of Prof.Sujoy B.Roy. The department has developed excellent facilities for clinical Care and has fully developed facilities for adult and pediatric interventions including coronary interventions, valvuloplastry, device closure of congenital shunts and electrophysiology services(including radiofrequency ablation, pacemaker, bi-ventricular pacemaker and cardioverter defibrillator implantation).

The Laboratory testing facilities includes treadmill testing, holter recording, echocardiography(including transthoracic, transesophageal, 3-D and fetal echocardiography). Tilt testing for syncope and electrophysiology testing including CARTO system mapping

The department has done pioneering work in the field of rheumatic fever and rheumatic heart disease incuding juvenile mitral stenosis, coronary artery disease including angioplastics, arrhythmias, cardiomypathies and congenital heart diseases.
